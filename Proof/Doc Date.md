# Doc Date

Document date is that date the document was produced, or created. This is mandatory field

If there is no document date, use the policy effective date as doc date, unless the policy effective date is in future date.  If the policy effective date is future we should capture batch date as doc date

Document date will be available at the corners of the documents most of the times.

**Keyword:** NOTICE DATE, PROCESS DATE, ISSUE DATE, COUNTERSIGN DATE, PREPAREDDATE, MAILING DATE, STATEMENT DATE, DATE OF INVOICE, BILLING DATE.
