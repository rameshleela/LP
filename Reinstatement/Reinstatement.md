# Reinstatement

Reinstate date
Payment received date, The date the policy is reinstated. but you MUST fill in either this field or Reinstate without Lapse on every reinstate document.
Keywords: policy Reinsted, policy continues with out interruption, policy in force with no lapse
However, most of the times that date will not always be available.
